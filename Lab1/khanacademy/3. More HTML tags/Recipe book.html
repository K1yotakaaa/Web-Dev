Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Time Serves
20 minutes 4

Recipe:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 4 large eggs
  • 150g pancetta or bacon2 cloves garlic
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté garlic, and cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl, mix with Pecorino and Parmesan cheese. Season with black pepper.
  4. Drain spaghetti and toss with pancetta. Remove from heat, add egg mixture, and stir quickly to form a creamy sauce.
  5. Serve immediately, topped with extra cheese and black pepper.

Teriyaki Chicken Bowl

Time Serves
30 minutes 4

Recipe:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) mirin
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) sake
  • 1 tbsp (12g) sugar
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2 cups (400g) cooked rice

Instructions:

  1. Mix soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, ginger, and garlic in a bowl to make the teriyaki sauce.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, cook chicken until golden and fully cooked.
  3. Add the sauce to the pan, simmer until thickened and chicken is coated.
  4. Serve chicken slices over rice, with steamed vegetables on the side.

Margherita Pizza

Time Serves
1 hour 2

Recipe:

  • 2 1/4 tsp (7g) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups (360ml) warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups (440g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp (5g) salt
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead into a smooth dough and let it rise for 1 hour in a warm place.
  3. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out the dough into 12-inch rounds.
  4. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, add mozzarella slices, and a few basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly. Serve hot.
